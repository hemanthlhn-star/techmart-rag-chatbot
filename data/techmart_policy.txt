

## About TechMart

TechMart was founded in **2010** and has grown into a trusted retail company with over **500 employees across India**. We are committed to providing high-quality products and excellent customer service nationwide.

---

## 1. Shipping & Delivery Policy

* TechMart delivers orders across India within **3 to 5 business days**.
* **Express delivery** is available in metro cities within **1 to 2 business days** for an additional charge.
* Shipping charges:

  * **Premium Members**: Free shipping on orders above ₹500
  * **Non-Premium Members**: Free shipping on orders above ₹1000
* Orders below the minimum value may incur standard shipping charges.

---

## 2. Return Policy

* Customers can return products within **30 days of purchase**.
* Requirements for return:

  * Valid purchase receipt
  * Product must be in original packaging
* Refunds or replacements will be processed after inspection.

### Special Condition for Electronics:

* Electronic items are eligible for return only within **7 days of purchase**.
* Items must be:

  * Unused
  * In original packaging
* Returns beyond 7 days for electronics will not be accepted.

---

## 3. Policy on Damaged Goods

We ensure all products are carefully packaged. However, if you receive a damaged product:

* Report the issue within **48 hours of delivery**.
* Provide:

  * Order details
  * Clear photos/videos of the damage
* After verification, we will offer:

  * Replacement OR
  * Refund (full/partial depending on the case)
* Damages caused after delivery due to misuse will not be covered.
* TechMart reserves the right to inspect the product before approval.

---

## 4. Payment Methods Policy

We offer multiple secure payment options:

* Accepted methods:

  * Credit/Debit Cards
  * Net Banking
  * UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
  * Digital Wallets
  * Cash on Delivery (if available)

* Key points:

  * Orders are processed only after successful payment confirmation.
  * Failed transactions will be refunded within **5–7 business days**.
  * Cash on Delivery may include additional charges.
  * Fraudulent transactions may lead to order cancellation.

---

## 5. Warranty Policy

* Warranty period varies by product and is mentioned on the product page or invoice.

* Covers:

  * Manufacturing defects
  * Functional issues under normal use

* Does NOT cover:

  * Physical damage
  * Misuse or accidents
  * Unauthorized repairs
  * Normal wear and tear

* Warranty claims require:

  * Proof of purchase
  * Product inspection

* Valid claims will result in:

  * Free repair OR
  * Replacement

* Final decision rests with TechMart after evaluation.
